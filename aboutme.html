About Me

I was born and raised in rural Kentucky. I spent most of my life on a farm in the small town of Bardstown, Kentucky. In highschool, I taught myself how to code in Python and TI Basic, and began to work on small personal projects.

Throught these projects, I got started on game development. I made a few small games, and then discovered roguelike games. The visuals and gameplay really appealed to me, and I dove into the genre.
